How it works

This app uses IP-based geolocation to estimate the location of a device (like a laptop). It queries a free service (ip-api.com) that maps IP addresses to physical locations using ISP records, regional registries, and other data sources.

Accuracy

  • Country level: ~99% accurate
  • Region/State: fairly good, varies by country
  • City level: 50–75% accurate globally
  • Street level: unreliable with IP alone

Results include an accuracy radius (e.g., 50 km), shown on the map as a circle.

Limitations

Accuracy is affected by VPNs, proxies, mobile networks, and corporate gateways. Sometimes the location may point to the ISP’s hub rather than the exact device.

Privacy & Safeguards

This demo does not store your IP address. Real-world tracking apps must respect user consent, data protection laws, and use minimal retention of personal data.
